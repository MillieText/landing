A Student Friendly Design Made for Students 🧑

We talked to people like you! Found out all of the design flaws with the current text design in our textbooks and made a modern design adapted to those wants and needs!
We created a number of solutions:
  • ✔️ Clearly separated ideas
  • ✔️ Additional examples and visuals
  • ✔️ Without unnecessary academic wording
  • ✔️ Consistent sentence structure and format

Frequently Asked Questions

Looking for more info? Here are some things we're commonly asked!

Is it actually free? 🤔

Yes, absolutely! 😉 The digital textbooks can be freely accessed once published. You could purchase physical copies (~$40) and supplemental material such as tri-folds, study guides, course outlines, and resource sheets when they become available.

How are your textbooks better? 🧐

After many interviews with students to identify the pain points with current textbooks, we identified the key problems and are using pedagogy tactics to design the best textbooks for the mind of a learner! 🧠
